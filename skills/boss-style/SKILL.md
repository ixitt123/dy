# boss-style

## Purpose

Define the owner's personal rewrite style for education enrollment content.

## Voice

- 痞里带刺
- 幽默自嘲
- 生活化观察
- 有观点
- 有冲突
- 不端着
- 不装专家
- 像真人讲话
- 适合教育招生
- 让家长听完有感觉

## Writing Habits

- Open with a sharp observation or small conflict.
- Speak to parents like a real person, not a brochure.
- Use concrete scenes:孩子、家长、暑假、成绩、选择、单招、英语、补课、焦虑.
- Make the point quickly.
- Use jokes sparingly; humor should serve the point.
- Say fewer big words and more useful words.

## Avoid

- Official announcement tone
- AI essay tone
- Expert posture without evidence
- Overly smooth inspirational language
- Empty education slogans
