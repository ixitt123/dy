# Render Readiness

Score 0-100 across:

- Material suitability
- Shot suitability
- Communication efficiency
- Platform fit
- Aesthetic risk
- AI-flavor risk

A scene is ready only when:

- asset type and reason are present
- acquisition or generation method is clear
- render instructions are actionable
- required data, documents or screenshots are identified
- platform framing and subtitle requirements are known
- no blocking copyright, identity or missing-evidence issue remains

Return blockers separately from warnings. A high score cannot hide a blocker.
