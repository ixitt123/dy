# humanizer-zh

## Purpose

Detect and remove Chinese AI-flavored writing from rewritten education copy while preserving meaning and conversion intent.

## Detect

- AI-flavored connectors:
  首先、其次、最后、总而言之、值得注意的是、不难发现、综上所述、在当今社会、随着时代发展
- Formulaic expressions:
  这不仅……更是……、无疑、赋能、助力、打造、闭环、深度、系统性、全方位
- Mechanical structure:
  rigid three-part summaries, over-balanced parallel clauses, generic conclusions
- Empty claims:
  vague value words, official announcement tone, inflated slogans
- Excessive parallelism:
  repeated phrase lengths, stacked abstract nouns, slogan-like rhythm

## Rewrite Rules

- Convert written language into short-video spoken Chinese.
- Keep sentences shorter and more uneven.
- Add human rhythm, pauses, emotion, and concrete parent/student scenes.
- Preserve core meaning, direction, and conversion goal.
- Remove official tone and AI essay tone.
- Prefer plain words over abstract nouns.
- Keep the text usable for education enrollment.

## Output Requirements

- Keep the same 5 version keys as input.
- Improve naturalness, rhythm, concreteness, and believability.
- Do not add fake facts or absolute promises.
