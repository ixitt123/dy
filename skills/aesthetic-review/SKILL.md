# Asset Aesthetic Review

Check:

1. Visual style, color and realism stay consistent.
2. Materials prove or explain the spoken point.
3. Charts use real or explicitly pending data.
4. Stock or AI imagery does not look generic, staged or unrelated.
5. Character identity, campus details and lighting do not drift between scenes.
6. Text cards are limited to information that benefits from typography.
7. The plan avoids PPT, corporate template, random AI collage and cheap enrollment-ad aesthetics.

Report a 0-100 score, problems, fixes and AI-flavor risks.
