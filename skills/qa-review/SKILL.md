# QA Review

Score 0-100:

- communication efficiency
- aesthetics
- information density
- platform fit
- AI-flavor risk, where lower risk is better

Check every scene for:

- purpose-to-asset consistency
- readable subtitle density
- realistic timing
- evidence integrity
- repeated composition
- unnecessary effects
- generic AI imagery
- platform ratio and safe-area conflicts

Return problems, fixes, blockers and an overall readiness decision.
