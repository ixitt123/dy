# Platform Strategy

## Douyin

- Vertical 9:16, high information density.
- Strongest evidence or conflict must appear within 3 seconds.
- Large subtitles, short shots, direct cuts.

## Video Account

- Vertical 9:16, moderate information density.
- Allow more complete explanation and warmer real-life materials.
- Subtitles remain clear but less aggressive.

## Xiaohongshu

- 3:4, clean composition and save-worthy information.
- Prefer infographics, checklists, documents and attractive real photos.
- Avoid loud sales graphics.

## Bilibili

- 16:9, longer explanation and stronger logical continuity.
- Prefer charts, screen demos, documents and multi-step visual evidence.
- Avoid forcing every scene into short vertical-video pacing.

Always return fit scores for all four platforms, even when one is the selected target.
