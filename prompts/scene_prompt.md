# Scene Prompt Rules

- Every scene has one clear purpose.
- Composition must name location, time, subject, foreground, middle ground, background, lighting and visual focus.
- Image prompts must describe a coherent frame in the requested ratio and style.
- Motion prompts describe camera movement and environmental movement only; they do not request video generation.
- Avoid generic phrases such as "high quality", "beautiful scene" or "a student" without concrete visual evidence.
- Vary shot scale and composition while preserving one visual system.
- Do not place a person in every shot.
