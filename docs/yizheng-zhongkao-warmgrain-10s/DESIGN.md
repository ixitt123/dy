## Style Prompt

Use HyperFrames' warm-grain template language: cream paper, visible grain, forest green, ochre, and terracotta blocks. Reframe the Yizheng middle-school entrance exam warning as a warm but firm parent reminder. The result should feel like a serious printed notice brought to life, not a cold tech dashboard.

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f5f0e0`
- Forest green: `#3b5e3a`
- Ochre: `#cc8832`
- Terracotta: `#c45d3e`
- Ink: `#2b2118`
- Muted ink: `#7a6248`

## Typography

- Chinese display and body: `"Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Number labels: `"IBM Plex Mono", "Cascadia Mono", monospace`

## Motion Rules

- Warm-grain card slides and gentle stat bubbles.
- Three beats across 10 seconds: exam scene, parent question, start-now action.
- Keep entrances readable and use the template's gentle drift.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use cold neon colors.
- Do not use pure black backgrounds.
- Do not add stock-photo imagery.
- Do not make text small or dense.
