## Style Prompt

A tense Chinese middle-school entrance exam warning video for parents in Yizheng. The visual language should feel like a campus gate at dawn, exam paper texture, and a countdown bulletin. It should be urgent and cinematic, but restrained enough for education content: pressure, responsibility, and immediate action.

## Colors

- Background: `#12100d`
- Paper: `#f0e5ce`
- Ink: `#241b16`
- Warning red: `#c9362c`
- Exam gold: `#d39b45`
- Muted text: `#c8b99d`

## Typography

- Chinese display and body: `"Microsoft YaHei UI", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Numbers and labels: `"IBM Plex Mono", "Cascadia Mono", monospace`

## Motion Rules

- High-pressure but readable pacing: sharp entrances, 0.28-0.55s.
- Use push and dark crossfade transitions between scenes.
- Use finite countdown pulses and exam-line sweeps.
- Keep text large and sparse; every phrase must be readable within 2 seconds.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use cheerful school colors.
- Do not use purple-blue tech gradients.
- Do not use stock-photo style blur or decorative cards.
- Do not make the message soft; the hook must feel immediate.
